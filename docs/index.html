Competitive Insights

Trilogy Care’s advertising emphasizes maximizing funding and transparent care management, leveraging both video and image creatives across Facebook and Google. While Trilogy’s messaging is clear and value-driven, competitors like Bolton Clarke and Just Better Care use richer lifestyle imagery and broader CTA strategies, potentially engaging a wider audience. Trilogy stands out for its funding efficiency message but could improve creative diversity and audience targeting.

Trilogy Strengths

+ Clear value proposition around maximizing care hours and funding efficiency
+ Consistent branding across platforms
+ Use of both video and image creatives

Gaps vs Competitors

- Limited creative diversity (mostly static images on Google)
- Narrow CTA focus compared to competitors
- Messaging is highly functional, lacking emotional/lifestyle appeal
- Potential gaps in targeting broader family decision-makers

Recommendations

Expand creative formats (carousel, lifestyle video, testimonials)
Competitors use richer visuals and storytelling, which increases engagement and emotional connection
Broaden CTA strategy to include 'Learn More', 'Support at Home', 'Book a Consultation'
Competitors drive more actions and engagement through varied CTAs
Target family decision-makers and carers in messaging
Competitors appear to target broader audiences, increasing reach and relevance
Monitor competitor claims for regulatory compliance
Ensure Trilogy’s messaging remains compliant and adopt best practices from competitors
